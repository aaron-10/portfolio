Graphics for Solar Boat Twente

2024

For the technical presentation at Solar Boat Twente, we needed some graphics and animations to support the story. I made a detailed textured model in Blender based on the Solidworks models, and rendered some animations that match the script.

It includes some exploded views, animations of moving parts, and shots of the boat in the water to show what kind of forces act on the boat and how they should be compensated by the controller.
