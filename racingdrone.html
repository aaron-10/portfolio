Racing drone

2024

This is a racing drone I built using mostly off the shelf parts. It runs Betaflight on the flight controller, which is quite common flight control software. It has a camera and a video transmitter, such that it can transmit a live video feed to FPV video goggles. This way you can fly the drone as if you're sitting in it. It has a top speed of more than 90km/h, quite scary...
